Frequently Asked Questions

How do you coordinate work across different time zones?

As a fully remote studio with team members worldwide, we've developed efficient asynchronous workflows while ensuring regular overlapping hours for collaboration and reviews. We work with clients from all regions and can adapt to their time zones for meetings and project coordination.

What information should I include when inquiring about a project?

It's helpful to include your project timeline, budget range, style references, and a brief description of your vision.

How do you handle project revisions?

We include a set number of revision rounds in our project quotes. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an agreed-upon rate.
