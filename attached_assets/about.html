Who We Are

At Cloma Production, we're a passionate team of animators dedicated to bringing stories to life through the magic of animation. From concept to final frame, we believe in crafting stories that entertain and inspire.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the animation industry with compelling, high-quality works that touch hearts across the globe.

Our Mission

We strive to push the boundaries of animation by creating original and innovative projects while providing opportunities for talented artists to thrive.

Our Story

Cloma Production began as a small team of passionate animators from different parts of the world with a shared dream. Working entirely remotely, our team was inspired by studios like Mappa and Madhouse, and we set out to create animation that could stand alongside the best in the world while telling diverse stories from global perspectives.

Today, we're growing into a vibrant online studio that connects talented artists across the globe, blending traditional animation techniques with cutting-edge technology to create visually stunning and emotionally resonant works that transcend cultural boundaries.
