About Cloma Production

We are a passionate team of animators, illustrators, and storytellers working remotely from all corners of the world. Inspired by studios like Mappa and Madhouse, we are committed to bringing diverse stories to the global animation scene.

Our goal is to create animation that resonates with audiences across cultures while staying true to our unique artistic vision and the global perspectives of our international team.
